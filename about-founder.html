MY JOURNEY

From Passion to Purpose

Yoga came into my life as a source of healing, strength, and clarity. It transformed not just my body but also my mind and soul.

My journey of self-discovery inspired me to learn, grow, and help others experience the same transformation.

With deep faith in the power of yoga and holistic living, I founded Celestial Yoga to create a peaceful space where everyone can reconnect with themselves and lead a healthy life.
